FAQ

Questions answered

What exactly do I get when I unlock a creator?
You get the creator's full name, Instagram handle (with direct profile link), personal WhatsApp number, and their media kit — which includes past brand collabs, content samples, rate card, and typical turnaround time. Everything you need to start a conversation and close a deal the same day.
How are engagement rates verified?
Every creator goes through a 5-step check: (1) engagement rate calculated across their last 30 posts, (2) comment quality audit to filter bot comments, (3) follower growth pattern review to catch follow-unfollow behaviour, (4) audience spot-check for fake follower percentage, and (5) niche consistency test to confirm the creator's content actually matches their stated category.
Can I see creator data before unlocking?
Yes — completely free. Before unlocking, you can see every creator's niche, city, follower count, engagement rate, average reel views, average likes, average comments, audience age range, gender split, top audience cities, and pricing for reels, stories, and static posts. The only thing hidden is the creator's identity. You pay ₹149 only to reveal that.
What if the creator doesn't respond after I unlock?
Every creator on our platform has confirmed their availability before being listed. If a creator is unresponsive within 48 hours of your first message, write to us at hello@brandbae.in and we'll replace the unlock credit or issue a full refund — no questions asked.
Is there a subscription or monthly fee?
No. Brandbae is fully pay-per-unlock. You only pay when you decide to reveal a specific creator's contact details. There are no monthly fees, no platform subscriptions, no minimum spends. Browse all 200+ creators and their full stats completely free, forever.
I'm a creator — how do I get listed?
We're currently onboarding creators in batches. If you have between 1,000 and 100,000 followers, consistent niche content, and genuine engagement, you can apply to be listed. Listing is completely free for creators — we charge brands, not you. Send your Instagram handle to hello@brandbae.in and we'll be in touch within 48 hours.
